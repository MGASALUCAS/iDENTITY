Who are we?

we take care of your healthy life

Promedical is an online platform equipped with range of features for health care giving using digital technologies to enable access of health information and services in a more and convenient way.

Welcome

Our Why

Our community has insufficient health facilities, medical supplies and health workers are in huge demand.To bridge that gape we are inspired to start promoting health care to the community around us by making well-informed choices and enable access of health services in a simple and more digitalized way to reduce the gap.

Our Mission

To bring better and low cost health care services remotely to all people using online consultation and medical test at home.
To enable access of health information through online discussion forums while also mapping health centers and their services in google Map.

Our Goals

To promote healthy lives and promote well-being for all using digital technologies.
To ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health-care services, including for family planning, information and education, and the integration of reproductive health.
